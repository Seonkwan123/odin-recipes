Mac and Cheese Recipe

This recipe will show you how to cook something that is not known to be a elegant dish, turn into just that: One-Pan White Cheddar Mac and Cheese. This recipe will show you how to be create a delicious mac and cheese bowl and feed all your hungry family. Bueaty of Mac and Cheese is that, you can eat it for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Just make sure to portion control, as this dish will be packed with calories, and you will gain weight.

  • 16 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 ½ cups milk
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup margarine
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 ½ cups shredded white Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese, divided
  • ¼ cup panko bread crumbs
  • ¼ cup real bacon bits
  • 1 tablespoon snipped chives
